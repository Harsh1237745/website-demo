About Techify

The foundation of Techify started in 2009 by two researchers, including Harshit Waghmare, who discovered the Principle of Smart Power.
This principle showed that technology could be powerful without wasting massive amounts of energy.

Our Mission

We are driven by a simple mission: to deliver innovative and high-quality tech products that genuinely enhance your daily life. We believe the future of technology should be accessible to everyone, not just the experts.

That's why our focus is always on reliability and thoughtful design. We select and create products that are simple to use, built to last, and offer true value, ensuring the technology you buy just works.

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted source for smart, reliable technology. We see a future where our thoughtfully designed devices make life easier and better for every customer.

We aim to connect homes and lives seamlessly, making Techify the first choice for simple, future-forward electronics around the world.

Our Values

We focus on three main things: Keep it Simple, Be Honest, and Help You Do More. We work hard to make our technology easy to use (Simple). We promise to be open and upfront about everything we do (Honest). Most importantly, we want our products to truly make your life better (Help You Do More).
